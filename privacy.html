ToyGround 3D

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

ToyGround 3D is a premium learning app made for children ages 1.5 to 7+. We built it to be a safe, private place to learn and play. This policy explains, in plain language, how we handle information.

We do not collect any personal data.

Information we collect

None. ToyGround 3D does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your child. We have no accounts, no sign-up, and no login.

No tracking or advertising

  • No third-party advertising or ad networks.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No device identifiers are collected or shared.
  • No social networking, chat, or messaging features.

Where your data lives

Everything your child creates and unlocks — toys, drawings, coloring pages, stickers, and progress — is stored locally on your device. It never leaves the device and is not sent to us or anyone else.

Purchases

ToyGround 3D offers a single one-time purchase (Full Access, $1.99) to unlock the app. It is not a subscription and does not auto-renew. All payments are handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details.

Children's privacy

ToyGround 3D is designed for children and complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws (such as GDPR-K). Because we collect no personal information from anyone, including children, there is nothing to share, sell, or disclose. Purchases, external links, and settings are placed behind a parental gate.

Third parties

We do not share information with third parties because we do not collect any. The only third party involved is Apple, solely to process the optional in-app purchase under Apple's own privacy policy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.

Contact us

Questions about privacy? Email us at glassioapp@gmail.com and we'll be happy to help.
