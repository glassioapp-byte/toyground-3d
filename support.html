Frequently asked questions

How much does the app cost?

ToyGround 3D unlocks with a single one-time purchase of $1.99. There are no subscriptions, no recurring fees, and no hidden charges. You buy once and keep everything forever.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the unlock screen and tap Restore Purchase. As long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID, your Full Access unlock will be restored at no extra cost. It's also covered by Family Sharing.

I bought Full Access but the app is still locked.

Make sure you're connected to the internet and signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase, then tap Restore Purchase. If it still doesn't unlock, email us and we'll sort it out quickly.

Is the app safe for young children?

Yes. There are no ads, no tracking, no accounts, and no chat or social features. Purchases and external links are placed behind a parental gate. All progress stays private on your device.

How do I get a refund?

Purchases are handled by Apple. You can request a refund directly from Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Which devices are supported?

ToyGround 3D works on iPhone and iPad running a recent version of iOS/iPadOS.
